universe.py
-----------
Defines the stock universe used throughout the thesis backtest. It contains a
manually curated dictionary of the 20 largest US companies by market cap for
each year from 1997 to 2024 (selected at Dec 31, used as the portfolio for the
following year). It then downloads historical daily Close prices for all unique
tickers (1992-2025) via yfinance and saves them to universe_prices.parquet. The
S&P 500 index (^GSPC) is downloaded separately as the benchmark and saved to
benchmark_price.parquet. After downloading, the script runs a data quality
report that flags tickers missing entirely, with too few trading days, with a
late start relative to their required training window, ending early, or
containing large mid-series gaps.
